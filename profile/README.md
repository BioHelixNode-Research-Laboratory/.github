## About Us

BioHelixNode Research Laboratory is a Liberia-based scientific training and research initiative focused on promoting bioinformatics, computational biology, genomics, biotechnology, and data-driven biological research among young Africans, particularly Liberians.

The organization was created to help bridge the gap between biological sciences and computational research by providing accessible learning opportunities, hands-on training, mentorship, and collaborative scientific experiences for students and young researchers.

Our mission is to support innovation, scientific capacity building, and locally relevant scientific solutions through beginner-friendly education, research exposure, and collaborative learning environments.

## Current Programs

### Summer Bioinformatics Internship 2026

A beginner-friendly online internship designed to introduce Liberian students and young graduates to:

* Introduction to Bioinformatics
* Linux and Command Line Basics
* Biological Sequence Analysis
* Python for Biology
* Genomics and NGS
* Biological Data Visualization
* Collaborative Mini Research Projects

The internship focuses on practical learning, mentorship, teamwork, and scientific communication.
